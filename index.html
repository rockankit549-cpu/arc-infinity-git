About Us

Leading Testing Laboratory

Established in December 2019, ARC Infinity Lab Private Limited has rapidly grown to become one of the leading testing laboratories in the region. With over 12,500 projects completed and more than 1,450 clients served, we have demonstrated our capability to deliver high-quality services that meet the diverse needs of our clients.

At ARC Infinity Lab, we pride ourselves on being a leading provider of comprehensive testing services for building materials including non-destructive testing (NDT), soil, pile, chemical testing, construction materials, and drinking and construction water.
